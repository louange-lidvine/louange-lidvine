## 🙋‍♀️ About Me

I'm a **Data Analyst & Business Analyst** with a background in software engineering, currently working at **Centrika Ltd** in Kigali, Rwanda. I specialize in turning complex operational data into clear, actionable business insights — using SQL, Python, Power BI, and Excel.

Before moving into data, I worked as a **frontend developer and team lead** at Rwanda TVET Board, where I built data-integrated web systems and led engineering teams. That background gives me a rare ability to understand both the data infrastructure and the business questions it needs to answer.

I care deeply about using data to improve **public services, healthcare systems, and operational performance** across Africa. Every dashboard I build, every report I automate, every query I write — it's all in service of helping organizations make better decisions faster.
